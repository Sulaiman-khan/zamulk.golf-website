Junior Golf Program

Foster a love for the game stays our goal number one. Kids loves to play, they will want to spend more time around the game, and will focus on ways to get better.

The motivation to get better is the main determining factor for improvement.

Incorporating games into practice, developing their confidence and competitive spirt early on will stay as our core value to inculcate in the game of golf.

Senior Development Program

Program format:

  • Includes six teaching sessions.
  • Has a duration of 60 minutes per session:
  • Socializing and feedback time x 15 minutes with tea and coffee
  • Includes time on the course in the last weeks of the program, accompanied by chief coach.
  • Provides an opportunity for participants to take advantage of a special offer that would encourage their transition to becoming a regular golfer.
  • Costs participants
  • Customized programs catered to your specific needs.
  • Short-term and long-term options.
  • Small group or 1-on-1 coaching options.
  • Progress Tracking & Frequent Testing.
